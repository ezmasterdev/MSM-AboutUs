NAME OF THE BUSINESS

The business name "Mmmm Sarap Milktea" is a phrase that translates to "Yummy and delicious milk tea". The "Mmmm" in the name, is an onomatopoeic expression that suggests a fulfillment in one's gustatory senses. The term "Sarap", is a Filipino root word for the term "masarap" which means "delicious". Lastly, the term "milktea" is just a combination of the words milk and tea to form the MSM business acronym. The "Mmmm" part is also a reference to the owner's family because each member has a name that begins with an "M" and there are precisely 4 members. The owner believes that a business that cherishes family, is a business worthy of success.

THE OWNER

Mmmm Sarap Milktea (MSM) "Start your day with a pop!" is a milk tea business founded by Mr. Marl Ezechiel C. Conde, a student from Centro Escolar Integrated School - Manila. He aspires to become a professional web developer and stock trader. He plans to pursue Computer Science as his course for college. However, life can often be unpredictable and it would not hurt to atleast have a backup plan for the future. Although it initially started as a requirement for his entrepreneurship subject, this aspiring student entrepreneur aims to manifest his vision for market dominance with MSM.

BUSINESS ORGANIZATION

Since the owner of the MSM business enterprise is a young aspiring entrepreneur, he didn't have the means to start a whole business by himself. Fortunately, the young entrepreneur is surrounded by people who are willing to invest in his visions. Among those investors are his parents, who genuinely wishes to contribute in the manifestation of the enterprise's success. For this very reason, the young entrepreneur decided to include his parents in managing the business. After all, both of his parents are extremely experienced in corporate life. Especially his mother, who has been an executive in multiple shipping agencies. Although shipping is a different industry, she is skillful enough to rapidly adapt and employ her skills in finance and management in the enterprise.
